    DJ Timmy Turner

    An experienced DJ ready to energize your event!

    Musician, producer, DJ, promoter, and electronic music enthusiast, DJ Timmy Turner hails from Malmö, Sweden. After humble beginnings playing at local nightclubs, he has held multiple residencies, expanded worldwide, and has crafted a variety of mixes. Originally inspired by EDM and funk artists from his native land, Timmy has finely tuned his sound through constant experimentation.

    Having opened for well-known DJs such as Tiësto, Pauly D and Fisher, Timmy Turner is no stranger to the limelight. His Summer 2019 tour, “I Wish I Had a DJ”, gained him a place on New House Magazine’s European DJs to watch list. Above all else, he is a purveyor of deep, infectious dance grooves and a feel-good atmosphere. Catch him as he plays bars, clubs and college campuses during his return to action this Spring.
